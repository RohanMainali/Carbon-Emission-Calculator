Are electric vehicles really helping the environment?

Electric vehicles (EVs) are revolutionizing transportation by offering a cleaner, more sustainable alternative to traditional gas-powered cars. Unlike internal combustion engines, EVs produce zero tailpipe emissions, significantly reducing air pollution and greenhouse gas output. Even when considering the emissions from electricity generation, studies show that EVs have a lower carbon footprint over their lifetime compared to conventional vehicles.
